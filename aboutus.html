About Us

Sambandh Health Foundation is an initiative by a group of family
members/ caregivers who came together to find solutions for their loved
ones living with schizophrenia and bipolar disorders and decided to share these solutions with people in a similar situation. It was registered as a charitable trust in August 2011.

The solutions are based on the strength of human relationships. These are relationships between:

  • A person living with mental illness and the family/caregivers;
  • A caregiver and another caregiver;
  • A person with mental illness and another person with mental illness;
  • A person with mental illness and a mental health professional;
  • A person with mental illness, their family, and community.

This web of relationships provides the community based supports which allow a person with mental illness to live a life of dignity and respect in the community.

We began by trying to understand the existing mental health services in India and in other countries. In the process we were introduced to a clubhouse model called Connections (Halifax, Canada). Connections uses ‘Framework of Support’ written by, and based on experiences of people who have recovered from mental illness and now living a wholesome life using community and professional supports to manage the illness, as their guiding manual.
